Welcome to Livestock Manager

Livestock Manager is a powerful mobile and web application to help save you time and effort and make managing your beef herd easier and more efficient. The average working day of a beef farmer is 12 hours. When time is added on for paperwork and record keeping it is closer to a fourteen hour day. Here at Livestock Manger we promise to reduce the workload and offer an interactive application that is easy and intuitive to use.
